* PdfView *


The structure of text file containing bookmarks:
[TAB[TAB]...]BookmarkText/PageNumber
...
[TAB[TAB]...]BookmarkText/PageNumber


Example:
1. Chapter one/2
	1.1 Chapter one.one/5
	1.2 Chapter one.two/7
		1.2.1 Chapter one.two.one/8
		1.2.2 Chapter one.two.two/10
	1.3 Chapter one.three/15
2. Chapter two/26
3. Chapter three/41
